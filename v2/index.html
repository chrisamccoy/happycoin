Storecoin is a new public blockchain project for franchise and chain store companies to create their own programmable currency.

Storecoin is also a blockchain API for developers to build the next generation of cash registers, loyalty systems, micropayment reward systems, last mile delivery network engines, and more for the world’s largest hub & spoke-based organizations.

How StoreCoin Works

StoreCoin is a Proof of Network-mined blockchain for chain stores, franchises, and affiliate-based organizations to launch their own cryptocurrency and their own developer blockchain on top of.

Imagine in the not-so-distant future a McDonaldsCoin, a StarbucksCoin, a CrossFitCoin, and more.

These NewCoins will be built on top of the StoreCoin app coin platform.

Through the StoreCoin Developer API, the currency in these NewCoins can power cash registers, e-commerce systems, staff bonuses, payroll, last mile delivery networks, and even contracts with vendors for the world’s largest franchise and chain stores.

NewCoins will be mined when the Franchise or Chain Headquarters buys them at market value, then releases them through Store Apps (sApps) in technology properties connected to the StoreCoin Developer API.

NewCoin transactions simultaneously write to its own blockchain and to the StoreCoin blockchain. 

NewCoins and StoreCoin developer APIs are interoperable.

The StoreCoin protocol is mined with a 5% Network Fee from all minted NewCoins. 

This is called "Proof of Network". 

The circulation only grows if there is usage on top of its app coin platform. The more usage, the more valuable that StoreCoin will be. This is a closed loop network effect.

StoreCoin mints tokens called “STORECOINS”. Investors can buy, sell, and trade STORECOINS across exchanges. NewCoins will be available on exchanges, too.

StoreCoin development is funded by its own Token Sales and from an additional 5% Network Fee paid by the mining of NewCoins.

StoreCoins will be minted at their fair market value daily at 9am Pacific Time. 

StoreCoin is an R&D project of Footprint -- enterprise messaging and social networking for franchise and chain stores, set to launch in 2018. 

StoreCoin has its early team in place in addition to its first API Developer in Footprint -- the enterprise messaging and social networking platform for the world’s largest franchise and chain store companies that is set to launch in 2018.

Reservations are now being taken for its upcoming Seed Round Token Sale. StoreCoin is now in its Alpha Development phase. StoreCoin is expected to be in production with its first set of NewCoins and sApps by 2019.

Why Storecoin

There are many benefits to Storecoin:

Granularity

Bitcoin isn’t divisible or flexible enough to support the granularity needed for micropayments.

Stability

A separate token like STORJ provides a layer of protection from the volatility of larger markets.

Scalability

Using STORJ helps us identify and fix problems at scale, paving the way for a larger, more stable network.

Incentivization

We pay for our community's hard work performing tasks like tier-one tech support and development with STORJ.

Micropayments

A network funded hub would allow for quick transactions, as well as keep transaction fees low for the users.

Partnerships

Storj Labs is dedicated to building long-lasting partnerships and supporting the broader [Ethereum] ecosystem.

Ecosystem

Users can earn STORJ for sharing disk space, then use it as renters themselves, creating a positive feedback loop.

Future Growth

We plan to use the token in ways raw Bitcoin or Ether can’t. For example, the Storj-specific signaling described in SIP0002.
