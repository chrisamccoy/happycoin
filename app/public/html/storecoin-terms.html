EXHIBIT A
Description of the Company, Storecoin Blockchain, and Tokens
Storecoin the Company, a Delaware Corporation co-founded by Creator and CEO Chris McCoy
with the help of CTO Raghavendra Kidiyoor, DevOps Jay Pal, and Blockchain Architect Pablo
Yabo, is an R&D subsidiary of Footprint Labs, a Delaware company committed to bringing
enterprise chat to franchise, chain stores, and affiliate-based organizations,
Storecoin, Inc. will offer a Storecoin Blockchain available worldwide and will introduce a Token
of utility for that Storecoin Blockchain known as Storecoin. The Storecoin Blockchain is
intended to allow hierarchy-based organizations to privately share revenue across parent and child
networks with no fees using smart contracts with a privacy option.
Storecoin is designed to act as a network incentive for blockchain miners and stakers, consensus
pools, application developers, hierarchy-based organizations, token holders, users of blockchain-
based applications, and more to enable free and private parent-child revenue sharing among other
features.
Holders of the Storecoin token are granted rights to contribute to a larger application and services
ecosystem system rather than holding an investment interest in Storecoin itself.
Once created, Storecoin will be based on the Ethereum protocol and conform to the ERC-20
standard. Prior to its public network going live, the Company will create and deploy an Ethereum-
based smart contract, which creates and maintains a ledger that maps Ethereum addresses to Token
balances and implements this ERC-20 standard. In the future, the Company may migrate
Storecoins to another protocol such as Storecoin itself, EOS, Rootstock, or more.
