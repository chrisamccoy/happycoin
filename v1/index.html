Storecoin is a public blockchain for systems governed by hierarchy and by revenue sharing.

These include franchises, chain stores, sports leagues, affiliate-based organizations, and more.

Storecoin is an Ethereum-family technology and is fully compatible with the Ethereum public network. If you can run a Dapp on Ethereum, you can run it on Storecoin, too.

Storecoin will be compatible with EOS, too.

What We Believe

The $4 Trillion global franchise industry, the $18 Trillion chain business industry, and the $xx billion sports industry will eventually be decentralized and tokenized.

The Storecoin blockchain will power it.

The Problem

Hub & Spoke-based systems are governed by complex revenue sharing agreements.

Oftentimes, the commission or royalty needs to be private.

Public blockchains and simple Smart Contracts do not solve for this -- especially for micropayments.

Our Solution

Storecoin solves this problem by enabling private, automated revenue sharing inside smart contracts.

Also, by enabling private revenue sharing across multiple addresses and unlimited levels of hierarchy.

With zero transaction fees to the sender.

Why This Matters

Entirely new Dapps can be invented to power technology and customer loyalty systems for HQ2B, hierarchical organizations.

For example, every time a transaction occurs through a Dapp, the Organization HQ receives a guaranteed commission.

HQ gets a royalty on everything.

Target Customers

  • Organizations where HQ manages a network of stores, branches, or affiliates (HQ2B).
  • Business systems who disburse money to their customers or clients
  • Organizations where there are multiple levels of revenue sharing
