How StoreCoin Works

Storecoin is a blockchain protocol for systems governed by hierarchy.

These include franchise and chain stores -- industries where +$20 Trillion dollars of goods flow through every year.

The Storecoin Developer API will one day power the cash registers, customer loyalty programs, micro bonuses for staff and stores, supply chains, invoicing workflows, last mile delivery networks, drone delivery systems, and more for parent-child hierarchical business systems

The Storecoin ledger can be public or private. It can support hierarchical, parent-child transactions.

The Storecoin cryptocurrency powers mining and usage of any App built on top of its developer API.

Storecoin guarantees automatic payments [stores] to any identity called in the Storecoin API. This includes Headquarters, locations, staff, and even customers.

Storecoin guarantees [stores] to the parent network -- most often Headquarters -- for API calls of any identity called in its active child network.

Storecoin is an Ethereum-family technology and is fully compatible with the public Ethereum network. If you can run a Dapp on Ethereum, you can run it on Storecoin too.

Storecoin has its first major developer partner in Footprint -- enterprise chat for franchise and chain stores.

Why Storecoin

There are many benefits to Storecoin:

Granularity

Bitcoin isn’t divisible or flexible enough to support the granularity needed for micropayments.

Stability

A separate token like STORJ provides a layer of protection from the volatility of larger markets.

Scalability

Using STORJ helps us identify and fix problems at scale, paving the way for a larger, more stable network.

Incentivization

We pay for our community's hard work performing tasks like tier-one tech support and development with STORJ.

Micropayments

A network funded hub would allow for quick transactions, as well as keep transaction fees low for the users.

Partnerships

Storj Labs is dedicated to building long-lasting partnerships and supporting the broader [Ethereum] ecosystem.

Ecosystem

Users can earn STORJ for sharing disk space, then use it as renters themselves, creating a positive feedback loop.

Future Growth

We plan to use the token in ways raw Bitcoin or Ether can’t. For example, the Storj-specific signaling described in SIP0002.
